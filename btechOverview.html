📘 B.Tech Overview

The Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) is an undergraduate program that provides a strong foundation in engineering and technology, combining theoretical concepts with practical applications.

⏳ Duration and Eligibility
Duration: 4 years (8 semesters)
Eligibility: 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (PCM)
Admission: Based on entrance exams like JEE Main/Advanced, state-level exams, or institute-specific exams
