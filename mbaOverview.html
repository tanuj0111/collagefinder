📘 Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

📘 BBA Overview

The Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) is a 3-year undergraduate degree that provides students with a strong foundation in business management, entrepreneurship, and leadership skills. It covers subjects like marketing, finance, HR, and operations.

⏳ Duration and Eligibility
Duration: 3 years (6 semesters)
Eligibility: 10+2 in any stream from a recognized board
Admission: Based on merit or entrance exams conducted by universities or colleges

🚀 Future Scope

BBA graduates can pursue careers in marketing, finance, sales, human resources, and consulting. Many find entry-level managerial roles in both public and private sectors.

It also lays a strong foundation for higher education like MBA or specialized programs in finance, international business, or digital marketing.

The BBA program equips students with practical knowledge and soft skills required in the corporate world.
