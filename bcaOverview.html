💻 Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA)

📘 BCA Overview

The Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) is a 3-year undergraduate degree program focusing on computer application development and programming skills. It serves as a foundational course for careers in IT and software development.

⏳ Duration and Eligibility
Duration: 3 years (6 semesters)
Eligibility: 10+2 in any stream (preferably with Mathematics or Computer Science)
Admission: Based on merit or entrance exams conducted by various universities

🚀 Future Scope

BCA graduates have opportunities in software development, web design, data analytics, IT support, and more.

Many students pursue higher education such as MCA, MBA, or specialized certifications in cloud computing, cybersecurity, and AI.

The BCA course is designed to provide practical exposure to software and IT industry practices.
